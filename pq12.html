Al Weaver (born January 3, 1981) is an English actor and writer. Weaver is known for his role in Nintendo as Rex in the game Xenoblade Chronicles 2 released in December 2017. He reprised the role for the character's appearance in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, as well as the trailer revealing Pyra and Mythra as playable characters.



He is also known for Colette (2018),Press (2018), and Peterloo (2018).
